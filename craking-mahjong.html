Meet Sharon

Hi friends — so glad you're here.

Mahjong has been one of the great joys of my life, and now I get to do my favorite thing: teach it to others.

Whether you've never touched a tile or you're brushing up, my classes are about making the game easy, fun, and welcoming. No experience needed — just curiosity and a little patience with yourself.
